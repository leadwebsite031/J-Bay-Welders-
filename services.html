Coastal Maintenance Guide

The salt air in Jeffreys Bay is relentless. Without proper maintenance, even the best steel will fail. We don't just build—we help you protect your investment.

Stainless Polishing

Our specialized stainless steel polishing removes surface contaminants and restores the protective chromium oxide layer, essential for J-Bay's coastal climate.

Industrial Priming

We apply zinc-rich cold galvanizing primers to all mild steel welds. This provides sacrificial protection, preventing rust from creeping under the paint.

On-Site Repairs

Found a rust spot? Don't wait. We provide on-site reinforcements and repairs to catch structural issues before they become expensive replacements.
