About Garth

Garth is a professional welder known for his attention to detail and love for providing high-quality workmanship. With years of hands-on experience in industrial and residential steel fabrication, Garth founded J-Bay Welding to bring a standard of excellence to the Kouga region that residents can rely on.


Whether it is hand-crafted security gates, heavy-duty trailer repairs, or specialized stainless steel polishing, every project is treated with a focus on durability, safety, and a clean, professional finish. At J-Bay Welding, we don't just join metal; we build solutions that stand up to the South African elements.
