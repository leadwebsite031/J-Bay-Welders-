Rust Prevention

The J-Bay Salt Challenge

Living within 5km of the Jeffreys Bay coastline means your steel is under constant attack from salt-laden air. Without professional maintenance, structural failure isn't a matter of "if," but "when."

Stainless Steel Polishing

Is your stainless steel showing brown spots? This is known as "Tea-Staining." It doesn't mean the steel is low quality; it means the protective layer has been compromised by salt buildup. We specialize in high-grade mechanical polishing that removes oxidation and restores the metal's natural resistance to the elements.

Corrosion Protection

For mild steel gates and structures, we provide specialized cold-galvanizing primers and industrial-strength coatings. Our process ensures that your welds—the most vulnerable part of any structure—are sealed against moisture and salt.

Yearly Inspection

We check for hairline cracks in welds where rust starts its journey.

Chemical Cleaning

Removal of deep-seated salt contaminants before they pit the metal.

Protective Buffing

A specialized finish that makes it harder for salt to "stick" to the surface.
